APPLICATION OF AI ART

(1) ENTERTAINMENT AND ART CREATION



One of the most prominent applications of AI art is for entertainment and creativity illustration. With AI art models like Mid-journey and Stable Diffusion, individuals regardless of their artistic skills can use the tool to illustrate and brainstorm their ideas, bringing their imagination to real life in just seconds. Moreover, some artists make use of the tool to generate a base image or structure for backgrounds of their art.

(2) COMPETITION



Some level of prompt writing skills are required to generate good images. Some big companies like Coca Cola launched an AI art competition where participants can take part in writing a prompt and generating art for the company. These types of events are to emphasize human cognitive experiences in collaboration with machines.

(3) FILM AND TELEVISION PRODUCTION



Movies, especially animated ones, can take up a long period of time to complete. Recently, a short film called “The Dog & The Boy” (2023) has its background generated by AI. Additionally, filmmakers can use AI art tools like storyboard.ai to generate storyboards for their films. It provides film makers with precise angles and perspectives that are incredibly useful to use as reference in films and television.

(4) ADVERTISING



AI-generated visuals and designs can be utilized in advertising campaigns, branding, and marketing materials. Nowadays, famous brands such as Nike and Dior use AI generated advertisements as well. This can add realism to the brand product images.
